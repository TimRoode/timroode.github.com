What to Expect

A Rolf Session typically lasts 80 minutes. For men you will wear underwear or exercise shorts and for women underwear or exercise shorts and a sports bra. I will assess your posture and movement at the beginning and end of the session. During a session, I will apply pressure to the myofascial tissue using my hands, fingers, arms, and sometimes elbow to carefully move the tissue.
